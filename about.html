Our Story

At Carolina Lumper Service (CLS), we provide more than just labor—we offer efficiency, reliability, and strategic workforce solutions to keep warehouse operations running at peak performance. CLS was built on the principle that strong client partnerships lead to better productivity, smoother operations, and long-term success.

Before launching CLS, Steve Garay co-founded and managed a company specializing in container unloading and warehouse staffing on a national scale. Recognizing that rapid expansion often came at the cost of client relationships, he stepped away to create a company dedicated to prioritizing quality service and strong partnerships over growth for its own sake.

With years of experience in logistics, workforce management, and automation, Steve and Dani have built CLS into a trusted provider of high-quality labor solutions—offering flexibility, precision, and accountability to warehouses, distribution centers, and supply chain operations across the region.
